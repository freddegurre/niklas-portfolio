justGuts

... Is an interdisciplinary collective formed by Fiona Berry, Szabolos Frioska, Niklas Gustafson, Ursula Illet, Tiki Muir And Harry Whitelook.

Our individual works and interests span from ceramics, printmaking, carving, performance, moving image and everyting in between. Together, we aim to re-imagine ways to exhibit, make and experience art. Literally and figurativley speaking.
